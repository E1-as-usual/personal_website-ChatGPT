Contact

Send a brief, a question, or an idea.

The form sends your message to contact@chiurciu.com. For large files or 3D models, use direct email and attach the files from your own email app.

What helps in a good brief?

Dimensions, files, reference images, material, deadline, approximate budget, quantity, and desired finish level.

For STL, 3MF, OBJ files or reference images, send them by email after the first contact.

Send direct email

3D Modelling + rendering

Include dimensions, sketches, references, the purpose of the model, whether rendering is needed, and how many final images you want.

3D Printing

Include the STL/3MF/OBJ file if you have one, preferred material, quantity, approximate size, and whether you need finishing.

Photography

Mention the type of work, location, date/period, approximate number of images, and how the images will be used.

Small-scale building

Describe the material, dimensions, use case, finish level, and whether you have visual references.

CV / professional

For professional opportunities, mention the context, role, time period, and what information you need.
