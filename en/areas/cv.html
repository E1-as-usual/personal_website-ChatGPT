Professional summary

I am currently studying architecture at the “Ion Mincu” University of Architecture and Urbanism in Bucharest, with an expected graduation in 2027. I am particularly interested in projects that combine spatial thinking, visual representation, material understanding, and practical construction processes.

My experience spans architectural modelling and rendering, 3D printing, photography, woodworking, construction, and restoration work on traditional timber roofs and heritage structures, alongside roles involving organisation, communication, and coordination.
