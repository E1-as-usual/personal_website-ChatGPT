Extra

Rendering as an add-on to 3D modelling.

When rendering makes sense

Rendering is not treated as a separate service. It is an extra for modelling projects that need presentation images, atmosphere, lighting, materials, or final compositions.

Simple rendering

A reasonable premium over modelling for clear presentation images without a complex scene.

Presentation rendering

For lighting, materials, chosen camera angles, and a more carefully composed image.

Advanced rendering

For atmosphere, details, post-processing, and multiple frame or material variations.

Rendering is quoted only together with modelling or with an existing usable 3D model. Cost depends on number of images, detail level, materials, lighting, scene complexity, and number of revisions.

What influences the price

Brief clarity

Shape complexity

Fine details, organic forms, or parts with tolerances increase the working time.

Rendering as add-on

Prices are indicative, not final quotes. Where ranges exist, the lower end is used as a starting point, but costs may vary depending on complexity, iterations, detail level, and requested renderings.

Process

A simple path from idea to usable model.

1. Brief

We define the model purpose, dimensions, references, level of detail, and whether renderings are needed.

2. Estimate

You receive an indicative time and cost estimate, including rendering if it is part of the project.

3. Modelling

I build the model and send captures or previews for checking.

4. Delivery

I deliver the files and, when applicable, rendered images for presentation.
