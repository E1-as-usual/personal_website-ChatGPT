Services

From print-ready file to finished object.

Printing from client files

For clean files that are already properly prepared for printing. This is the simplest and most affordable option.

File checking and adjustments

File checking, small corrections, orientation, simple repairs, or more careful preparation when the model requires it.

Modelling + printing

For ideas that do not yet have a 3D model. Pricing depends on how clear the brief is and on the required work time.

Finishing

Sanding, priming, painting, and assembly, quoted separately depending on size, detail, and the desired finish level.
