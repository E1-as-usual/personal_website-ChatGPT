Privacy

Data and contact

This page briefly explains what information is collected through the contact form, file uploads, and occasional update subscriptions.

Contact form

When you submit the form, your name, email address, project type, message, and any attached files are processed. This information is used to reply to your request.

Uploaded files

Small files may be sent as email attachments. Larger files may be stored temporarily in a private server area and accessed through a secure link. File links expire automatically after the period configured on the site.

Newsletter and updates

If you explicitly choose to receive occasional updates, your email address is saved in a private list. You can unsubscribe using the unsubscribe link included in future messages.

Administration and security

Contact data, uploaded files, and the subscriber list are stored in private areas of the server. Email and admin passwords are not exposed in public website pages.

Data contact

For questions about data sent through the site, write to contact@chiurciu.com.
